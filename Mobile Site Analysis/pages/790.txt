high price tag. Like many other smartphones, newer models of the iPhone are swathed in a high-impact glass. It gives them cool inductive charging capabilities, but the glass back puts them at risk for extra damage. Apple iPhone cases are, surprisingly, some of the easiest to buy. They’re widely available, and unlike many other phone companies, iPhone protective cases are sometimes interchangeable between phones. Models like the iPhone 6, 6s, 7, and 8 will all take the same phone cases, giving you extra flexibility in your purchasing options. Soft Shell iPhone Cases It’s a good idea to keep your smartphone in a durable case. To preserve the original look of your iPhone, a soft shell case offers basic drop protection with a bit of sleekness. Look for a soft shell case in silicone or leather for drop protection without extra bulk. A slightly firmer case with a raised edge is a great way to protect your screen at the same time: raised lip adsorbs impact if your phone falls face-first, and helps prevent scratches if you put your phone face-down on a table. Most hard and soft shell models make great “waterproof cases” provided that you have a waterproof model of the iPhone, but some materials (like leather) should be kept dry. TPU and gel iPhone cases offer slightly less protection, but will still adsorb some impact in the case of a bump or fall. Look to these gel cases for see-through protection. Hard Shell iPhone Cases for Extra Durability If you’re prone to phone accidents, then a hard shell case is the way to go. While thin, brittle hard shell cases are a good option (they’ll break upon impact so that your phone doesn’t have to), a heavy-duty hard shell iPhone case will adsorb fall after fall. The best phone case for the accident-prone is something with dual-layer protection: a hard outer shell with a soft silicone bumper inside. These cases will add significant bulk to your smartphone, but can take significantly more impact than a basic hard or soft shell case. iPhone Protective Cases with Extra Features If your phone does it all, it isn’t too much to expect that your phone case will, too. iPhone wallet cases, folio cases, and pop top cases offer protection and coverage—plus something extra. Wallet, flip, and folio iPhone cases are a great option for those who either carry just their phone or toss their phone into a larger bag. They’ll protect your phone screen when not in use, and different options give you a space to store your cash in or fold into a media stand. Similarly, pop top cases offer both protection and (especially for larger phones) a more secure grip. After all: why just protect from breakage when you could reduce the number of falls your phone sees altogether?
