Kitchen & Dining Room Furniture for Sharing Suppers & Stories Kitchens and dining rooms are not only places for cooking and eating. They’re spaces for congregating and connecting, savouring shared meals and stories, and spending quality time with the ones you love. With a setup as simple as a kitchen table and chairs, you can create an inviting space where family and friends can gather for morning coffee, afternoon tea, or anytime chatter. Shop kitchen and dining room furniture at Best Buy to transform the eating and cooking areas in your home into your favourite spaces. Dining Room Furniture Dining rooms are designed for, well, dining. But with the existence of TVs and distractions, dining rooms are often neglected. Bring back the traditional Sunday dinner with the right dining room furniture and rediscover the fine art of conversation. Dining Sets There’s a certain joy to gathering around the dining table over good food, plentiful wine, and non-stop banter. Whether you live in a cozy apartment with an open plan living space or a spacious house with a dedicated dining room, there’s a dining set to fit your space, style, and budget. Choose a complete dining set for a cohesive look, or mix and match a dining table and dining chairs to create your own custom style. A cute three-piece bistro set will find its place in a condo or breakfast nook, while a roomy rectangular dining table will afford space for dinner parties and family gatherings. Bar Tables and Stools With a bar table and bar stools, you can recreate the atmosphere of your favourite watering hole – in your own home. Not only do a tall bar table and stools create a casual look and feel, they’re also space-conscious in design, making them great for a basement bar or unused condo corner. And when you’re really short on space, bar stools also make an ideal addition to a kitchen counter or island. They can turn a simple cooking space into a welcoming gathering place for eating and conversing. Kitchen Furniture Besides the usual appliances and built-in cabinetry, a kitchen can benefit from a few extra pieces of furniture. Here is some kitchen furniture that can help organize any cook’s space. Kitchen Islands and Carts When cupboard space is scarce and prep areas are few and far between, a kitchen island or cart can be a saviour. Whether it’s rolled into a corner or placed front and centre, a kitchen cart offers an additional surface for slicing and dicing, as well as shelves or cupboards for tucking away dishware, spices, and other kitchen essentials. Some kitchen carts double as serving trolleys, so you can go from juicing lemons to serving cocktails -- all on the same unit. Buffets and Cabinets Dishware and glassware deserve their own home. So, what better place to house them than in a buffet or cabinet? A sophisticated yet functional addition to a kitchen, a buffet is often outfitted with cabinets and shelves for storing tableware, serving platters, and even bottles of wine. Traditionally, buffets also serve as a counter for serving food, but they can also be topped with your favourite decorative pieces. Want More Info?
