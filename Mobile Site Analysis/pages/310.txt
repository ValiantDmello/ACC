LifeProof Smartphone cases Your phone is your most accessible and most-used tech. It goes through all the transitions of your day and life with you, and can sometimes suffer the consequences of being used so frequently. Thankfully, LifeProof lives up to its namesake by giving you literally life-proof products that will keep your most important device in top shape. LifeProof has a highly accessible range of Smartphone cases available on Best Buy, ranging from nearly all models of Android phones and iPhones. LifeProof iPhone and LifeProof Android cases are made to fit your smartphone like a glove, providing optimum safety for your most coveted device—your smartphone. LifeProof Nuud and LifeProof FRE Waterproof Design LifeProof Nuud and Lifeproof FRE models are both hard-shell cases that make it possible for your Smartphone to be submerged in water for up to 2 metres and for up to an hour without sustaining any damage. These two models of LifeProof cases come with a screw-in seal for the earphone jack that makes it completely waterproof when used underwater. However, if the swimmer wants to listen to their music underwater, they can simply use waterproof headphones that can be plugged into a short waterproof extension that is also to be screwed into the headphone jack. LifeProof Next and LifeProof Slam Sleek and Durable Cases LifeProof’s NEXT and SLAM lines provide ideal protection with the addition of sleek and subtle design. Both these lines are compatible with various iPhones and Android phones, so everybody wins. The LifeProof Next is drop proof for up to 2 metres, resistant to dust, dirt, sand, and other kind of debris. It’s also Snow Proof to snow and ice which means it can protect your phone on nearly any adventure! LifeProof Tablet Cases In addition to LifeProof Smartphone cases, this brand also produces tablet and iPad cases that are equally as durable, protective, and come in appealing design. Unlike your Smartphone, your tablet can suffer different types of damage, including being thrown around in a bookbag or backpack, or undergoing the wear-and-tear of being around a house and used by multiple people. With widely compatible and highly protective designs, no matter what make or model of device you use, LifeProof will ensure you get the most out of it.
