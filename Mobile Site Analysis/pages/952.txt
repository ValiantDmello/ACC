makes some of the best audio equipment in the world; a difference in quality you can truly hear Sennheiser Over-Ear & On-Ear headphones You have no doubt seen the iconic Sennheiser logo on a legion of professionals. Sennheiser is known as one of the world’s foremost brands when it comes to high quality, professional-level headphones. Designed to be comfortable and long wearing, Sennheiser on-ear headphones can help you focus on making or listening to music. If you prefer over-ear designs, Sennheiser over-ear headphones give you a bit more room to really wrap your head in that all-encompassing sound. Much of Sennheiser’s headphone lineup is wireless, using Bluetooth connectivity to keep you linked to your music. Unlike some other headphones you’ll find, Sennheiser Bluetooth headphones aren’t prone to dropouts or static. Sennheiser truly wireless headphones While Sennheiser Momentum True Wireless headphones are getting all the press these days when it comes to true wireless headphones, they’re not the only game in town. Yes, even though the Sennheiser Momentum is one of the products Sennheiser is best known for (thanks to its supreme portability, noise-cancelling technology and superior comfort) you can still choose from numerous other Sennheiser in-ear headphones too. Sennheiser has been making truly wireless headphones and truly wireless earbuds since before most of us were even onto this technology. With comfortable designs, longer-than-average battery life and the ultimate in sound quality, you know you’re making a wise buying choice with a pair of Sennheiser wireless headphones. Sennheiser Sport headphones Whether you’re hitting the gym, running the treadmill or the trail, or just trying to zen out with some yoga, Sennheiser’s variety of sports headphones can fill whatever your need. Many Sennheiser headphones are splash and sweat resistant and some use a behind-the-ear band for a more secure fit. Plus, with long battery life, Sennheiser’s premium audio engineering has created the perfect choice for music lovers with an active lifestyle. Sennheiser gaming headsets Take your gameplay to the next level with Sennheiser gaming headsets. This lineup of ultra-comfortable, wearable headsets comes with built-in advanced noise cancelling microphones, so you can chat with your online buddies clearly—and stay comfortable for hours of battle. Sennheiser’s gaming headsets are known for their ability to nix acoustic lags for improved audio and video sync. Sennheiser Microphones Everyone from professional broadcasters, to audio-visual technicians and even YouTubers knows that when you need a proper, high quality microphone, you turn to Sennheiser. A Sennheiser microphone in front of you is a sure sign you’ll get top quality sound when it matters most. When you’re podcasting or doing an important presentation, you know you really only get one shot to get it right. That’s why the pros use Sennheiser mics. No matter what your audio need is, you’ll never go wrong choosing a top quality Sennheiser microphone, or headphones. Sennheiser has the right audio solution for ever need.
