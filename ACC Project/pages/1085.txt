That’s not all though, PlayStation is not only for gaming! With Sony PlayStation consoles you’ll also have access to all the most popular digital streaming applications—such as Netflix for movies, Spotify for music, and so much more. You can watch videos on YouTube, check in on your favourite Twitch streamer, or even add to your digital library with the hottest new releases in film, music, and television from the PlayStation Canada online store. Sony PlayStation is truly the one-stop solution for all of your gaming and digital entertainment needs! The evolution of PlayStation When the first Sony PlayStation console hit the market it pushed the boundaries of 3D gaming, set the standard for gamepads with the PlayStation controller design, and carved a path for years of new home console innovations to come. Today the Sony PlayStation 4 still leads the pack with intuitive new features and gamer-friendly design. The PlayStation 4 launched with trailblazing capabilities such as built-in recording, streaming, and sharing technology that has allowed players to easily save, edit, stream, and share their gameplay with the global gaming community in real time. PlayStation is also always on the cutting edge of performance—delivering consoles with 4K resolution, incredible frame rates, and the overall incredible visual experiences its fans are looking for. PlayStation’s enormous and diverse catalog of games The library of games available to play on the Sony PlayStation platform is vast, with something to offer for every type of gamer out there. PlayStation is home not only to all the biggest competitive shooters and free-to-play battle royale titles, but also to a diverse and critically acclaimed family of games ranging from indie darlings to epic AAA single-player story experiences. There are plenty of all-ages titles and party games for the entire family to enjoy, simulations for every major sporting league and more, terrifying horror titles for mature audiences, and everything in between. There is truly something to suit every taste in gaming on Sony PlayStation. Amazing accessories for PlayStation consoles There are plenty of wonderful peripherals and accessories that can really take your PlayStation experience to the next level. As previously noted, the PlayStation 4 is currently the only home console that has virtual reality support with Sony’s PlayStation VR. You can also bolster your gaming pleasure with add-ons such as the PlayStation Camera, an external hard drive for additional game and media storage space, or a high quality gaming headset capable of delivering crystal-clear stereo surround sound, just to name a few. You can even choose your own style with a wide variety of PlayStation controller colours, customizable skins, console faceplates, and more. Whether you’re playing the hottest new games or streaming the latest in binge-worthy TV, movies, and more, Sony PlayStation is your home for all the best in digital entertainment.
