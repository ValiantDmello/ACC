night means that you’ll spend about 1/3 of your life in bed? That’s a lot of bed, so it’d better be ideal. Thankfully, Best Buy offers a variety of beds and bedroom furniture, including mattresses, dressers, nightstands, and more, to keep you cozy and comfortable in your sleeping sanctuary. Beds and Mattresses Depending on the size of your room and the furniture in it, choosing the right bed, headboard, footboard, and mattress is key. Smaller rooms will benefit from a comfy twin bed or double bed with a slim-style frame, while larger rooms may afford the luxury of a king-sized bed. And for the kids, bunk beds are perfect since they’re typically designed with smart, kid-friendly features. Dressers and Nightstands Once you’ve got your bed all picked out, you can gussy up your bedroom with matching accent furniture. A simple dresser will keep all of your folded clothes off the floor, while a dresser hutch will add extra shelving. To hold the book you’re reading, an alarm clock, or a glass of water for bedtime, a nightstand is an ideal choice. Storage Solutions To keep your bedroom essentials neat and organized, a bookcase and shelves might do the trick. A slim bookcase will hug your wall, so you can store a whole lot of items (yes, even books) without taking up too much floor space. And with a variety of shelving options, you can make the most of unused wall space to showcase items and keep things off the floor.
