Pioneer is one of the top names in audio and has a reputation for delivering both innovation and premium sound Founded in Japan over 80 years ago, Pioneer’s first product was a speaker, and the company continues to be a leader in home audio and automotive speakers. The company has a history of industry firsts, including the first component stereo system, the world’s first car CD player and the first ever car-based GPS navigation system for the consumer market. Pioneer turntables, Pioneer controllers and headphones are prized by DJs, while Pioneer home theatre receivers are a considered best-in-class. Pioneer car audio systems are among the most popular upgrades over factory installed stereos. Home Audio & Home Theatre Pioneer home theatre receivers offer the ultimate in immersive audio, whether you’re viewing the latest Hollywood masterpiece, or enjoying your favourite streaming audio playlist. With a Pioneer receiver, you can expect premium quality components, and life-like sound reproduction. They feature latest technology such as Dolby Atmos, 4K video with Dolby Vision and HDR 10, and Bluetooth streaming and Wi-Fi connectivity with support for streaming music service like Spotify. Add Pioneer home speakers (such as the popular Andrew Jones designed line) for an awe-inspiring setup. For smaller spaces, a Pioneer stereo micro system delivers high quality sound in a compact, space-saving package. Pioneer Car Audio Pioneer car audio is a showcase for the company’s many innovations, and one of the most popular products for Pioneer Canada. Upgrade to a Pioneer car stereo and your car will quickly become your favourite place to listen to music. Pioneer introduced many firsts to automotive audio and you’ll find that innovation reflected in today’s Pioneer car audio and Pioneer double DIN multimedia systems, including the popular NEX series. Built around a high-performance Pioneer amplifier, they include features like integrated GPS, Bluetooth, HD radio tuner and SiriusXM-satellite radio capability. Many Pioneer in-dash multimedia systems include CD and DVD playback on big, sharp, LCD displays, while the latest generation includes a touchscreen with support for Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. Add a Pioneer subwoofer for thundering bass that will make your car rumble. Pioneer Headphones & Pioneer DJ Controller Pioneer’s reputation for audio excellence extends into the world of professional music playback. Over-ear Pioneer headphones are an iconic symbol of DJ culture. Pioneer turntables are a must-have for serious DJs, as is the centre of any professional sound system: the Pioneer DJ mixer. The pros don’t get all the fun: Pioneer also offers headphones and earbuds for consumer use as well. When you require the top name in audio, with best-in-class home theatre receivers, the best car audio lineup, and the equipment prized by professional DJs, look to Pioneer.
