Brother printers When you have a Brother printer on your home or work desk, you know you’ll always have a reliable, easy-to-use printer. Depending on what type of use you’ll have for your new printer, there are a few different choices. For the small office or home office, there is the all-in-one Brother HL monochrome laser printer series. It’s a multi-function printer and copier that offers a wireless connection to your smartphone and computer and has multiple functions including scan, fax, print, and copy. For the home, Brother Inkjet printers print in colour and black and white and have built-in Wi-Fi so you can print from all of your connected devices. You can also use your Cloud connection to print or scan from your Cloud accounts like Google Drive or Dropbox. Brother Ink and Toner With Brother toner and ink cartridges you’ll be able to hit the print button for all of your important documents. Brother toner will ensure each page you print on your laser printer will be clear and smudge-free. Toner cartridges are high yield so you can print approximately 1200 pages before you need to swap one out again. Brother ink is available in colour and black for Brother inkjet printers. It’s bright, colourful, and fade resistant, letting you choose individual ink cartridges or a Brother 3-pack of ink to print approximately 600 pages before you’ll need a new cartridge. Brother label maker You’ll be able to create professional-looking labels with a Brother label maker. There are a few different types of label maker to suit different kinds of users. Brother Desktop label makers have QWERTY keyboards, different fonts and font styles, and built-in memory to store dozens of your label designs. Brother P-Touch label makers have one-touch operation and built-in templates so it’s easy to make labels for the home or office. If you need a speedy label maker for the office, Brother Direct Thermal Monochrome label makers will print up in high resolution at a rate of up to 93 address labels per minute. For a wireless label maker, Brother P-Touch CUBE label makers feature Bluetooth and let you wireless print from your iPhone or compatible smartphone or connect to your computer via USB cable. For all Brother label makers, you can buy paper tape refills so you’ll be able to keep printing as many labels as you need. For your small business or home needs, Brother has everything you need to print, scan, fax, and label.
