Get inspired Read more The Best Gift Ideas for Any Dad Father's Day is fast approaching and you probably want to get something special for the special guy on your list. That might be your own dad, husband, brother, son, close friend, or other influential father in your life. Ditch the boring necktie, socks, gift card, subscription box, or cologne this year and get dad something really cool that will put a smile on his face. Not sure what to get? Check out these great ideas that will suit every type of dad. Gifts for New and Expecting Dads One of the most exciting Father's Day a man will ever experience is his first one. This might be right after his new baby has been born or when he has a baby coming soon. Welcome him to the New Dads Club with a thoughtful gift like a man-friendly diaper bag that is designed like a backpack or messenger style so he can be ready to go with baby. Dad might also love a high-tech baby monitor he can tinker with. Gifts for Dads Who Love the Latest Tech If dad is into the latest tech trends, consider grabbing him a smart display he can use in his room, home office, basement, or den to control other smart devices in the home, listen to music, watch videos, make video calls, view photos, and get answers to queries. If he loves to sit back with his favourite tunes, consider a good pair of wireless headphones, bonus if they're also noise cancelling so he can fully immerse himself in his music. And give him a new turntable so he can dust off and listen to those old records he has stored away. You might also consider an upgraded phone so he can have the newest model of whatever device platform he prefers. Gifts for Gamer Dads You might think gaming is reserved for teens and tweens, but dads are pretty prolific gamers, too. If the dad on your list loves to sit back and immerse himself in a good RPG or other game, look at a gaming console headset he can use to chat with friends and others as he plays online. If you really want to surprise dad, get him the latest and greatest console, like a PlayStation 4 Pro or Xbox One S, so he can amp up his gaming experience. Don't forget to check out the latest hot new release title video games and grab one that will get him excited to log on and shut off from the world. Gifts for Home Theatre Dads There's no dad on Earth who wouldn't appreciate a new big screen TV for his living room, den, or man cave. Make sure to go Ultra 4K HDR, and help him get better sound with a smart soundbar that offers improved audio over what's built into the TV. If you don't have the budget to spring for a new TV, consider a media streaming box that will make dad's existing TV smart so he can access all of his favourite streaming services and content. If dad has been building out his man cave, look at home theatre seating options for maximum comfort, including reclining chairs with cupholders that will make movie-watching at home a theatre-like experience. Gifts for Fitness Dads A fitness tracker is a great gift for just about anyone. But for a dad who is into working out, cycling, running, or just wants to keep an eye on his general health and wellness, including heart rate and sleep, it's a perfect gift. You might also want to consider grabbing him some gym equipment so he can work out at home. First Father's Day Gifts If dad is celebrating his first Father's Day, you'll want a gift that's extra special and sentimental. Invest your time in loading photos onto a digital photo frame and present that to dad so he can relive memories before adding new ones to the mix. Speaking of which, grab him a fancy new mirrorless camera that he can use to capture lasting memories of his new baby. Last Minute Gifts For Father's Waited until the last minute? If you're looking for a great last minute gift for dad, grab a smart speaker he can use for music playback, smart device control, queries, and more. Or look into an item tracking device, like a Tile, that attaches to keys and other valuables in case he misplaces them – perfect for forgetful dads. Gifts From Baby or Kids to Dad If the child isn't yet old enough to sculpt their own pen holder or design a personalized card, grab something you can address from them to dad that comes from the heart. Maybe it's a new guitar dad can use to play songs for the little one, a fancy new watch, or a shiny new tablet so dad can binge-watch shows during those late night feeding sessions.
