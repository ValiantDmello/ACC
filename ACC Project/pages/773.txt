Reviews) $6.98 $6.98 Marketplace seller Show more Read more Spice Up Your Phone with Stylish LG Phone Cases Smartphones are your entry to the digital world—on the go. They’re a way to tell the time, text your family, and book your next vacation. But of the many things that they do, there’s one thing that they aren’t: impervious. Like any other smartphone, LG phones thrive in a phone case. The right LG case will extend your phone’s life and open your phone up to new features. Hard Shell LG Phone Cases For those of us who routinely drop their phones, a hard shell case is a must-have. These firm cases tend to be a little thicker, with a solid exterior that’s designed to protect your phone from drops and other impacts. Slimmer hard shell cases will break on impact in the case of a major fall—which is awesome, because it means that your phone isn’t breaking. Despite being designed for style (and a smoothness that can slide right into your pocket), they offer substantial protection … but they might need to be replaced eventually. If you need to upgrade your protection even more, look for an LG case that’s not just hard shell, but shock absorbent. These hard shell cases are reinforced with silicone, rubber, or even carbon fibre to adsorb even the toughest of impacts. They have a chunkier, more rugged design, but are a great choice for the super-clumsy or those who work out in the field. LG Phone Cases that Do Even More If a drop-tested phone case doesn’t do it for you, an Android LG phone case with extra features might be what you’re happier with. Look for options with extra grips, RFID protection, or even a discreet extra battery hidden in the back profile of the case. Battery cases are a great way to get even more out of your LG smartphone throughout the course of the day. They’re a must for anyone who’s on their phone while on the go, and are excellent for travellers or avid social media users. Most cases will hold one full charge, but if you search for it, a select few will charge your phone two or three more times throughout the day. Flip and Folio Cases for your LG Phone Slim hard- and soft-shell cases may be the most minimal option for your smartphone, but they’re not necessarily the most minimalist. An LG flip case or folio case can streamline not only your phone case, but also your entire accessory set for the day. If you want to leave your bag at home, look for an LG phone case with a folio cover and credit card slots. Some models also offer a kickstand, RFID protection, or a wristlet chain for added benefit. Smartphone cases are integral for keeping your phone in tip-top shape for as long as possible. Don’t miss the chance to select one that fits your lifestyle perfectly.
