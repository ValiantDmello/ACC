more Read more Stylish and Functional Samsung Cases There are few things worse than a cracked phone screen—especially if your phone isn’t under warranty. But even with great accident coverage, a broken phone is a big inconvenience in your daily life. To keep your screen (and everything else!) safe from most scrapes, bumps, and falls, a Samsung phone case can go a long way. Skins and Soft Shell Samsung Phone Cases Samsung is one of the biggest brands on the market when it comes to today’s smartphones. Not only are their phones popular and prolific, but they’re also quite literally some of the biggest. With generously-sized Samsung phone screens, it can be difficult to talk yourself into adding even more width to your smartphone. Soft shell cases are a great direction to take your larger smartphone body. Look for soft-shell Samsung cases for protection against falls, and choose models with descriptors like “fitted” in their name for an even slimmer profile. Soft shell cases are frequently made out of a soft, thin layer of silicone or plastic, both of which give the added benefit of an extra-grippy texture. Traditional, sticker-style smartphone skins offer little protection against bumps and scrapes, but “skin cases” will adsorb just as much impact as any other thin case. They’re designed to be contoured to the curves of your phone, fitting snugly for a slim profile. Gel and silicone skins are typically the most shock-adsorbent. Get Extreme Shock Protection with Hard Shell Samsung Phone Cases If you work hard and play hard, you may need an extra layer of coverage from your Samsung smartphone case. It’s easy to find Samsung Galaxy cases and Samsung Galaxy hard shell phone cases with a hard shell—or with a double-layered hard shell exterior and soft interior bumper. Hard shell phone cases will give you maximum protection against drops and scrapes, and a slightly raised front bumper will protect your phone screen from breaking. For ultimate protection in an ultra-slim format, look for fitted hard shell Samsung phone cases. These models offer the security of a hard shell case, while discreetly tucking in a soft inner layer close to your Samsung phone. The soft bumper around your phone absorbs drops while the hard outer layer takes the force of an impact, keeping your phone safe even in the case of a fall onto the floor. Coverage and Closure in Samsung Galaxy Cases For those seeking an alternative solution for their Samsung Galaxy phone case, folding and folio cases offer front coverage and an on-the-go stand. For a phone model this well-loved, the options are close to endless!
